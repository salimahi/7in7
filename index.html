The Practice

A competition worth entering. A practice worth keeping.

7 in 7 is a monthly screenwriting competition and creative practice built for writers who want a consistent outlet. A prompt, a deadline, and substantive feedback every cycle. Cash prizes and a produced podcast episode for those who place.

⏱

A Deadline Every Month

A new prompt drops the 7th of each month. You have exactly 7 days to write a 7-page script. The constraint is the point.

📝

Real Feedback

Every entrant receives substantive written feedback scored across 7 categories, whether you place or not.

🏆

Win & Get Produced

Cash prizes go to the top three finishers. First place also earns a produced episode of the 7 in 7 Podcast, a shareable portfolio piece.

The Cycle

How it works

Every month runs on the same schedule. Sign up once, and at 7pm ET on the 7th you receive the prompt and your unique submission link together in one email.

7th

Prompt & Link Delivered

At 7pm ET, registered entrants receive one email containing the monthly prompt and their unique submission link. Your 7-day writing window begins.

14th

Submissions Close

All scripts must be submitted via your unique link by 7pm ET. PDF format, up to 7 pages, no author name in the document.

15–21

Judging & Feedback

Experienced judges score each script across 7 categories and write individual feedback for every entrant.

22nd

Results & Feedback Delivered

Winners announced. Every entrant receives their written feedback by this date.

+30d

Podcast Episode Published

The winning script is produced and published as a full podcast episode within 30 days of results.

The Podcast

The 7 in 7 Podcast

Each month's winning script is performed by voice actors and published as a full podcast episode, distributed on all major streaming platforms. It's where writers and actors come together around your story.

  • A real, shareable artifact for your portfolio
  • Voice actors from across the country bring your characters to life
  • New casting opportunities open every cycle for actors looking for material to work on. Join the casting call mailing list.
  • The winner is invited to participate in the recording (optional)
  • Published within 30 days of results
  • Builds a growing public audio archive of short film writing
  • Your script stays yours. Submitting grants us only the right to potentially produce it as a podcast episode. All other rights remain with you.
