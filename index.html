The Competition

Built for writers who want to write.

7 in 7 is designed to fill a gap in the short film writing community — a competition that's affordable, international, gives you real feedback, and produces something you can actually share.

Deadline-Driven

The Cycle

How it works

Every month runs on the same schedule. Once you pay, you get a unique submission link automatically.

7th

Prompt Released

The monthly prompt drops at 7am ET. Your 7-day writing window begins.

14th

Submissions Close

All scripts must be submitted via your unique link by 7pm ET. PDF format, exactly 7 pages.

15–21

Judging & Feedback

Experienced judges score each script across 7 categories and write individual feedback for every entrant.

22nd

Results & Feedback Delivered

Winners announced. Every entrant receives their written feedback by this date.

+30d

Podcast Table Read Published

The winning script is produced and published as a full podcast episode within 30 days of results.
