7 in 7
Podcast

Scripts brought to life.

Each month's winning script is performed by voice actors from the Richmond film and theater community and published as a full episode of the 7 in 7 Podcast. Every episode is a real, shareable artifact you can put in a portfolio.

How It Works

From script to episode

🏆

A Script Wins

The 1st place script from each monthly cycle is selected for production.

🎭

Actors Are Cast

Voice actors from the Richmond film and theater community are recruited and rehearse the script.

🎚

It's Recorded

The table read is recorded virtually via Podcastle. The winning writer is invited to participate.

📡

It Goes Live

The episode is published across all major streaming platforms within 30 days of results.
