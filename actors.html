Voice Actors

Act in the Podcast

Each month's winning script is performed by voice actors from around the country. Join the mailing list to be considered for upcoming productions.

Join the Casting Call Mailing List
  • 📜

    Voice acting for the Write 7 in 7 Podcast operates under the SAG-AFTRA MicroPod Agreement. Roles are available to both Union and Non-Union actors.

  • 🇺🇸

    Actors must reside in the USA.

  • 🎙

    Actors will be required to join virtually for a 1 hour recording session. They must ensure that they have a strong internet connection, can arrive on time, and record in a quiet environment with a good microphone.

  • 💵

    A $20 USD stipend will be paid for each participant per episode via virtual Visa card.
