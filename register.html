The Process

How it works

1
Sign up below Choose your commitment (single cycle at $25 or all 12 cycles at $240/year), fill in your details, and complete payment.
2
Prompt + link delivered at 7pm ET on the 7th On the 7th of the competition month, at exactly 7pm ET, you'll receive one email containing the writing prompt and your unique, single-use submission link.
3
Write your script in 7 days You have until 7pm ET on the 14th. Write a script of up to 7 pages in standard screenplay format. Your PDF must contain no identifying information. The title page should include the script title only, with no author name.
4
Submit via your unique link Upload your PDF through the link in your email before the deadline. The link is single-use and non-transferable.
5
Feedback & results by the 22nd Every entrant receives written feedback scored across 7 categories. Winners are announced and prizes distributed.
